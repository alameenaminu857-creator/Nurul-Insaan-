Who We Are

Nurul Insan reaches those forgotten and isolated in ghetto communities, restoring hope through kindness, charity, and collective action. Founded on compassion, social responsibility, and the spirit of giving, we focus on Ramadan fundraising and year‑round interventions that provide relief, dignity, and hope to vulnerable populations.
